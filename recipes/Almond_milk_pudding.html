Almond Milk Pudding

Description

There isn't much of a description...

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white sugar, or to taste
  • ½ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • ⅓ cup cornstarch
  • ¼ teaspoon salt (Optional)
  • 3 cups unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 teaspoon unsalted butter (Optional)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (Optional)

Steps

  1. Whisk sugar, cocoa powder, cornstarch, and salt together in a saucepan off the stove. Add 1/4 cup almond milk; whisk until smooth and foamy. Continue adding almond milk, 1/4 cup at a time, whisking after each addition.
  2. Place the saucepan over medium-high heat and cook, stirring constantly, until mixture comes to a simmer and starts to thicken, about 5 minutes.
  3. Remove from the heat and stir in butter until melted and smooth. Stir in vanilla, then pour pudding into a serving bowl. Cover and refrigerate until chilled, at least 30 minutes.
